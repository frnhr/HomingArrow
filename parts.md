#### Data Formats

    UTC Time: 064951.000: hhmmss.sss
    Latitude: 2307.1256: ddmm.mmmm 
    N/S Indicator: N: N=north or S=south 
    Longitude: 12016.4438: dddmm.mmmm 
    E/W Indicator: E: E=east or W=west
